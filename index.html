Kenny Lam's Japan Travel Blog

I love travelling in Japan. I have been there over three times. My family and I plan our own self-guided trips everytime. The transit system is very complex and crowded yet very convenient and can get you almost anywhere without driving your own car.

One thing the Japanese really do well is maintaining the perfect balance between their old historic sites and new innovative modern architecture. Another thing is that the japanese characters are sometimes very similar to chinese characters which helps me a lot when reading signs.

My Top 5 Japan Cities

In my opinion, these 5 cities are all 10 out of 10 in different ways but for ranking sakes, here they are.
